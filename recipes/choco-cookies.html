Chocolate Cookies

Description:

These delicious chocolate cookies taste like brownies!

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside! Fudginess = Happiness!

Ingredients

  • two cups of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups margarine (softened), or butter if you prefer
  • two large eggs
  • two teaspoons vanilla extract, for extra flavor
  • two cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • one teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch (1/8 teaspoon) of salt
  • one cup of chopped nuts: you can go with pecans or walnuts, to add some extra crunch

Directions

  1. Step One: Preheat the oven to 175Â°C
  2. Step Two: Cream sugar and margarine in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  3. Step Three: Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl. Add to the creamed mixture and mix until just blended. Stir in nuts. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  4. Step Four: Bake in the preheated oven until edges are set and centers are soft, eight to ten minutes. Let sit briefly before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
