Caipirinha

Description:

The Brazilian national cocktail, Caipirinha is one of the most recognizable cultural trademarks of Brazil, along with Samba and Soccer.

It's unbelievably easy to prepare, and refreshing like the sunset summer breeze on a Rio beach.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lime, quartered
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 1/2 fluid ounces cachaça (brazilian rum)
  • 1 cup ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Squeeze and drop lime wedges into a large rocks glass. Add sugar and muddle until juices release.
  2. Step 2: Pour in cachaça and ice. Stir well.
