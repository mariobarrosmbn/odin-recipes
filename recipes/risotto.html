Risotto

Description:

Requiring only one hour, serve your guests with this great dish!

Easy to prepare and requiring no more than one hour, this risotto recipe is a guaranteed success!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 2 cups Arborio rice
  • 4 cups boiling vegetable broth
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 medium leek, sliced
  • 1 fresh red chili pepper, sliced
  • 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 pound small scallops
  • 1 bag fresh spinach leaves
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh ground pepper

Step one:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of live oil in a large, heavy bottomed saucepan over medium-high heat.
  2. Pour in the rice, and stir until the rice is coated in oil and has started to roast, two to three minutes.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium and stir in one-third of the boiling vegetable broth; continue stirring until incorporated.
  4. Repeat this process twice more, stirring constantly. Incorporating the broth should take fifteen to twenty minutes in all.

Step two:

  1. While you are cooking the rice, heat the remaining tbsp of oil in a pan.
  2. Stir in the garlic, leeks, red chili, shrimp and scallops.
  3. Cook until the seafood is just beginning to turn color.
  4. Add the bell pepper and the spinach; cook until the seafood is opaque.
  5. Combine with the rice, and season to taste with pepper.
