P찾o de Queijo (Brazilian Cheese Loaf)

Description:

Brazilian Cheese Loaf is a crunchy, cheesy piece of deliciousness reminiscent of Brazilian countryside traditional breakfast.

It's made without wheat flour, which means they're gluten-free and a perfect snack for people who suffer from celiac condition.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup olive oil or butter
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups tapioca flour
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2/3 freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 190째C;
  2. Step 2: Combine olive oil, water, milk and salt in a large saucepan and place over high heat. Bring to a boil and immediately remove from the heat.
  3. Step 3: Stir in tapioca flour and garlic, stirring until smooth. Set aside to rest for 10 to 15 minutes.
  4. Step 4: Stir cheese and eggs into the tapioca mixture until combined; the dough will be chunky, like cottage cheese.
  5. Step 5: Drop the dough by 1/4 cup-size balls onto an ungreased baking sheet.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until the tops are lightly browned
